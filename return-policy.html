Return Policy

Our return policy is designed to be simple and customer-friendly.

1. Return window

Returns are accepted within 3–5 days from the delivery date.

2. Eligibility

  • Product must be unused and in original condition.
  • Tag/packaging should be intact (if applicable).
  • Customized items may not be eligible unless there is a manufacturing defect.

3. How to request a return

Use the Returns page to submit your order ID and reason, or contact us on WhatsApp.

4. Refund rules

Refunds (if approved) are processed after inspection and verification. Refund method depends on your payment type.

5. Replacement

If replacement is approved, we will ship the correct size/color (subject to availability).
